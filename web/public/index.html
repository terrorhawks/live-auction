Toorak's Finest New Home

Contemporary Perfection - As featured in Belle magazine.

Positioned high on the north side of one of Toorak's finest streets, this brand new Nicholas Day masterpiece is where visionary contemporary design meets an elite quality of construction and appointment. Featuring elegant, understated interiors by David Hicks and refined gardens by Paul Bangay, this solid brick mansion residence provides for an entertainer's lifestyle of absolute sophistication and luxury.

Complemented by stunning streamlined decor, soaring ceilings and state-of-the-art appointments throughout, the home comprises a formal sitting and dining room, North-facing casual living and dining precinct and grand study - all with brilliant fireplaces and flowing from an impressive entrance/reception hall. The home is further enhanced by a Calacatta marble kitchen with Gaggenau appliances and butler's pantry, alfresco dining area with full outdoor kitchen overlooking a manicured lawn and travertine-tiled pool, home cinema with walk-in cellar and bar, and gym room.
